Most people don't know that there are BEARS in SPACE

There are a few reasons a bear might venture to space

  • They may be hungry and searching for moon cheese
  • They may be going to verfiy that Pluto is indeed a planet
  • They may be seeking international fame

We asked a few bears what their top 3 space destinations would be, here are the answers

  1. Jupiter (because it looks cool)
  2. The moon
  3. Mars
