WeizWild Hybrid Experiments

Original Wild and WeizWild

The original Wild was a beer made using 2 Row Barley and wild caught yeasts floating around West Oakland in October of 2016. It was surprisingly fruity, slightly tangy, and definetly strange. Not quite good, but with compelling elements.

The WeizWild was the much more tasty mixture of equal parts of the Wild and the previous batch of kit Hefeweizen. It was lighter and less strange with fruity tangy overtones.

Second Generation WeizWild

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs 2 Row Barley
  • 0.35 oz Hops
  • 1 gallon water
  • 1 oz First Generation WeizWild Hybrid

Basic Process

  1. Crack/Smash barley (Originally I forgot this and did it later by smooshing the barley in the wort.)
  2. Brew wort at about 155 F for an hour and a half.
  3. Strain wort into brewing vessel
  4. Add First Generation WeizWild as starter culture
